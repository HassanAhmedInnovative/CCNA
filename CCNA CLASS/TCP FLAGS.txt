

Tcp flags:-

1 urgent pointer:- The first flag in the urgetn pointer flag.
		   It is used to idenitfy incoming data as urgent.

2 acknowledgement (ack) :- used to acknowedge the successful receipt 
		   of packets.

3 push:- exists to ensure that the data is given the priority  and is 	 processed at the sending or receiving end

4 Reset (RST) flag:-
when a destination reject source request and then send you a reply with the RST flag set.

5 synchronization (syn) flag:-
	SYN flag is initialy sent when establishing the classical 3-way handshake between two hosts

6 Finished (fin) flag

This flag is used to stops the virtual connections.
